A once-in-a-lifetime Maldives getaway to a spectacular resort on the uninhabited island of Machchafushi, on an all-inclusive basis with a sunset cruise and optional overwater villa. Refundable hotel - book with confidence

We are working with hotels to ensure that they comply with the public health regulations regarding COVID-19.

  • Blissful island idylls: Surrounded by the sparkling turquoise waters of the South Ari Atoll and adorned with distinctive timbered walkways and Colonial-style buildings, Centara Grand Island Resort & Spa Maldives is a barefoot paradise with a palm-fringed beach and shimmering lagoon. Lose yourself in utter tranquillity with a beach or overwater villa stay, made even more inviting by a chic aesthetic and unparalleled views.

  • Cuisine with finesse: With six restaurants and bars, you can experience something different every day at Centara. Each morning begins with a fusion of flavours at Reef restaurant, while Maldivian specialities and conjured up at lunch and dinner. At dusk, head over to Aqua for a delicious aperitif against the stunning backdrop of the sparkling blue lagoon. Romantic meals over the water also await, making every moment more special than the last.

  • Unrivalled spa sensations: Could there be anything more relaxing than an outdoor massage, to the sound of rippling waves and a caressing warm breeze? Combining ancient Thai traditions with modern rejuvenation practices, indulge in tranquil massages and treatments - using the finest products from Vichy - and let your cares wash away. Its unique approach to wellness (and unique overwater location) puts this spa heads and shoulders above the rest.

How to get there

This secluded island resort is 15 minutes away from Malé International airport by seaplane. Transfers are not included, and must be purchased directly with the resort at least 72 hours prior to arrival. For further information on transfers, please see ‘Offer details’).
